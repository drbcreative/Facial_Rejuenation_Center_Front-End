MORE ABOUT US

At Facial Rejuvenation Centre, we endeavor to help you achieve your desired result to restore function, symmetry and harmony to your eyes, face, neck, ears, skin, and nose through surgical and non-surgical treatments. We want you to feel and look your very best with a beautiful, youthful, but natural appearance. All of our services are tailored specifically to you and guided by the principles of medical ethics, compassion, professionalism and the highest quality of patient care.

The FRC team is led by the highly trained and accomplished Dr. Deirdre Leake. Expert in all aspects of facial cosmetic surgery, Dr. Leake is dual board certified by the American Academy of Otolaryngology/Head and Neck Surgery and board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.

She loves her team and works with each member to ensure all are aligned with her vision. When working with FRC, you can rely on the newest technology and most updated training. Dr Leake and her professional staff will thoroughly discuss facial structural corrections and skin enhancements designed to produce the results you desire. Together, we will work to give you a completely natural appearance that is individually attractive and supportive of your self-esteem and confidence.

We look forward to meeting you and encourage you to browse our website and schedule a consultation to have the look you desire. We look forward to serving you in the very near future!

CELLULITE ERASE

Even if you’re in amazing shape, you may have cellulite – the lumpy, dimpled flesh often found on the thighs, hips, buttocks and abdomen. At Facial Rejuvenation Centre, we can “erase” cellulite using the breakthrough technology of a heating device called TempSure Envi.

WRINKLE ERASE

From forehead lines and the “11s” between our brows, to crow’s feet and marionette lines, we can help you “erase” the signs of aging. We will customize a wrinkle “erase” plan which may include SkinPen, Aquagold, Morpheus8, the Fractional CO2 Laser, and more.

CREPE ERASE

Most common under the eyes and on the upper inner arms, crepey skin looks finely wrinkled like crepe paper, and may sag or feel loose. We have a myriad of treatment options to “erase” crepey skin, including TempSure, the Fractional CO2 Laser, and Renuvion.

SAGGY SKIN CARE

A natural part of the aging process, sagging skin in the lower face and neck is attributable to the loss of collagen and facial fat. At Facial Rejuvenation Centre, we offer a range of treatment options to “erase” sagging skin, including Accutite/FaceTite, Renuvion, and surgery.

FAT ERASE

AGE SPOT ERASE

Age spots, brown spots, red spots, etc. can detract from your look. Though sun exposure is often to blame, such spots naturally come with age. At Facial Rejuvenation Centre, we can “erase” brown/red spots with IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) and Chemical Peels.
